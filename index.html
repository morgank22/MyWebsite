The Hearts Card Game

The Game of Hearts is a common and popular card game where it involves at least four players and the main point of the game is you want to get rid of all your cards that are hearts because they will give you points and you want to have the fewest points as possible. Therefore, whichever suit is laid down first (Clubs, Hearts, Spades, or Diamonds) every other player must lay down a card of the same suit but if you do not have that suit then you can lay down any card (stategy wise: laying down a card that has the suit hearts would be smart because the player with the highest numbered card with the suit in the current pile has to take the heart card). At the end of each round, all points are counted and the first player to 100 points hundred loses. Each heart card is worth one point but the queen of spades is worth 13 points.

Technologies used: JavaScript and HTML


Akari Puzzles

Akari is a game/logic puzzle where there is a grid and you have place light bulbs in different parts to light up the whole board. The light bulbs can be placed in the corridor cells but cannot be placed in the clue or wall cells. When a light bulb is placed it will light up the grid in the left, right, up, and down directions and light bulbs cannot be placed in the direct line of sight of each other. Additionally, the clue cells will be numbered between 0-4 which will tell you how many light bulbs are around this clue cell. Once the right number of light bulbs are placed beside a clue cell it will be consider satisified. The way the player wins the game/puzzle is by lighting up the whole board correctly and satisifying all clue cells.

Technologies use: Java and CSS
